Brief description on Ghost of Tsushima

Sucker Punch Productions created the action-adventure game Ghost of Tsushima, which Sony Interactive Entertainment released. The player takes control of Jin Sakai, a samurai on a mission to defend Tsushima Island, in this game, which is set in 1274 during the first Mongol invasion of Japan. There is a sizable open area in the game that may be explored on foot or on horseback. When confronted by foes, the player has two options: use Jin's katana to fight them head-on or transform into "The Ghost," a famous fighter who kills opponents covertly.

Stance Gameplay

Although the fundamentals of fighting are simple, not every opponent you encounter will be amenable to your novice sword stance. Along the way in the game, you'll encounter different foes who bring more than just swords to the fight. You must master and use new sword postures in order to defeat them.

Stone

Stone stance is best against sword users

Water

Water stance can break through shields.
Brutes may weild a shield which makes them vulnerable to water stance.

Wind

Wind stance can pierce the defense of spear users.

Moon

Moon stance is effective against brutes.
Although different brute types are weak to other stances
but the moon stance is recomended

Landscape

The game's landscape is replete with vivid, lovely moments that are atmospheric, memorable, and stunning settings in both new and classic video game universes.
